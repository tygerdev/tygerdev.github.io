What Is Tyger

Tyger Sports Network is an online platform for sports lovers. The first phase of our company is providing service of connecting users with coaches for personal 1-1 training session. Providing flexibility for both users and coaches.

Find coaches for any sports, anytime, anywhere.

Learn from your training summary to improve your game.

Assess your game by the local sports communities after 10 sessions.

How It Works

1. Check your sports summary
2. Add session

Find a coach based on sports, location, and experienced level

3. Check the coaches profile and reviews
4. Chat with the coach
5. Confirm your booking
